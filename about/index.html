About Me

Calvin, serving behind Bible Mapped

Welcome, I’m Calvin, and I have the privilege of stewarding the vision behind Bible Mapped.

The Bible is often treated as if it floats above history. Important, certainly. But still abstract. And for many people, that can make Scripture feel distant.

But the Bible is not distant.

It is rooted in real places, real people, real culture, and real history. And when we begin to see that more clearly, its message becomes clearer too.

That is the heart behind Bible Mapped.

Bible Mapped exists to help people understand that the Bible is not a disconnected collection of stories. It is one unified story. A real story. And from beginning to end, it points to Jesus Christ.

Through maps, history, language, context, and visual storytelling, my goal is to help bring Scripture into focus in a way that feels clear, meaningful, and alive.

The people inside the Bible did not stand where we stand now. Abraham did not have the full story in his hands. Moses did not read the finished pages of Scripture the way we can.

But we can.

And that is a gift.

What we hold is not a vague spiritual tradition floating above history. It is a real story, unfolding through real people, in real places, across real time. And all of it points to Jesus Christ — the Creator, the center of the story, and the One who invites us to know Him.

My prayer is that Bible Mapped helps you see His Word more clearly, and through it, see Him more fully.

And if you have questions, I’d love to hear from you. You can reach out at BibleMapped@gmail.com. We may even make a video answering your question.

In Christ’s love,

Calvin
