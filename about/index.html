Good day, I’m Calvin — the heart behind Bible Mapped.

Bible Mapped was built from a simple belief: the Bible is not boring, vague, or disconnected from real life. It happened in real places, among real people, inside real history — and all of it points to Jesus Christ.

I believe every Christian is responsible for their own relationship with God. Too many people only see Scripture through someone else’s eyes, someone else’s voice, or someone else’s fear.

But getting to know someone you love is not a chore.

A relationship takes time. It takes attention. It takes curiosity. And I believe our walk with God is the same way.

That is why Bible Mapped uses a simple lens.

Where Who Why What

Where did this happen?

Who was involved?

Why did it matter?

What does it reveal?

But this formula is not only about biblical places.

It is also about us.

You are standing in your own place in life. That is your where. You have purpose with God. That is your who. Your love for God is your why. And who you are becoming in Him is your what.

God plays no favorites.

You matter. Your story matters. Your questions matter. And your relationship with God matters.

My goal is not to tell you what church to attend or what denomination to join. My goal is to make one thing clear:

Jesus is King.

Bible Mapped is built with an iron-sharpens-iron mindset. We do not know everything. We miss things. So challenge us. Ask questions. Test the claims. Help us keep building a better framework for believers who want to know their Bible and its history.

Know your Bible. Know its history. Know the King it points to.
