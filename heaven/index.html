THE GOSPEL MAP

The Gospel in One Story

Every path leads to the same question: What has God done through Jesus Christ?

Where we began

God Created Us

God made us to know Him, love Him, and live with Him.

What went wrong

Sin Separated Us

Sin broke our relationship with God. We cannot fix that separation by our own goodness.

The promise

God Promised Rescue

From the beginning, God promised that He would make a way back.

The Savior enters history

Jesus Came

Jesus Christ came into real history. He lived without sin, revealed the Father, and came to save sinners.

The center of the map

Jesus Died and Rose Again

Jesus died on the cross for our sins and rose from the dead on the third day.

Repentance and faith

We Respond

The right response is repentance and faith. Turn from sin. Trust Jesus. Follow Him.

The destination

God Gives New Life

Those who belong to Christ are forgiven, made new, and given the hope of eternal life with God.

TRUE BELIEF

What Does It Mean to Truly Believe?

True belief is not just knowing religious facts.

It is not just saying a prayer.

It is not just feeling emotional for a moment.

True belief means you trust Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord. You stop relying on yourself to be right with God. You turn from sin and place your faith in what Jesus has done through His death and resurrection.

A prayer does not save you.

Jesus saves.

Prayer is one way we respond to Him with faith.

RESPONSE

A Prayer of Faith

If you are ready to turn to Jesus, you can pray honestly to God.

You do not need perfect words. You need a sincere heart.

God, I know that I have sinned and that I cannot save myself. I believe that Jesus Christ died for my sins and rose again. I turn from my sin and put my trust in Jesus. Please forgive me, save me, and help me follow You. Amen.

If you prayed this sincerely, do not stop here. Begin following Jesus. Read Scripture. Pray. Find a faithful church. Talk to a mature Christian. Keep walking.

The Gospel is not just the way to heaven. It is the way back to God through Jesus Christ.
