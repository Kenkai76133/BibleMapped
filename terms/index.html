Terms of Use

Bible Mapped is built to help people explore Scripture with care, clarity, and respect.

Bible Mapped is built to help people explore Scripture with care, clarity, and respect. By using this site, you agree to use the content in a way that honors God, respects the work, and supports thoughtful Bible study.

Use the Site Respectfully

The content on Bible Mapped is provided for learning, study, teaching, and personal reference.

You may use Bible Mapped to better understand the where, who, what, and why of Scripture. You may share our content with others, quote small portions, and use it for personal Bible study, group discussion, or teaching.

Please do not copy, sell, repost, or misrepresent Bible Mapped content as your own.

Content and Accuracy

Bible Mapped seeks to present Scripture in context through geography, history, culture, language, timelines, and biblical meaning.

We work to keep the content thoughtful and accurate, but Bible study often involves interpretation, translation, and historical context. Some topics may include different views among faithful Bible readers, scholars, and teachers.

Always compare what you read with Scripture, pray for wisdom, and continue studying.

Our Purpose

Bible Mapped is not built around secret knowledge or hidden teachings. Our goal is to share a perspective, a lens of context, that helps Scripture become clearer, deeper, and more connected to real people, real places, and God's real work in history.

We are learners too. The goal is not to replace Scripture, but to help people slow down, study carefully, and see the Bible more clearly.

Sharing and Teaching

You are welcome to use Bible Mapped ideas for teaching, discussion, and encouragement, as long as you do so respectfully.

When sharing larger portions of our content, please credit Bible Mapped. Do not alter our content in a way that changes its meaning or presents something falsely as our view.

Faith and Interpretation

Bible Mapped is created from a Christian perspective and is centered on the belief that Scripture is inspired by God and points us to Jesus Christ.

Our content may include biblical interpretation, historical explanation, maps, timelines, cultural background, and theological reflection. We encourage every reader to study Scripture carefully and seek understanding with humility.

User Contributions

If you email us, message us, or share ideas, questions, corrections, or resources, you understand that we may read, respond to, learn from, or use those ideas to improve Bible Mapped.

Please do not send content you do not have the right to share.

No Misuse of Content

You agree not to use Bible Mapped content to mislead others, promote false claims, attack people, or present the work in a way that is hateful, dishonest, or harmful.

Our goal is to help people study Scripture with truth, love, and respect.

Changes to These Terms
