Terms of Use

Bible Mapped is built to help people explore Scripture with care, clarity, and respect.

These Terms of Use explain how you may use Bible Mapped, including our articles, maps, timelines, images, summaries, studies, videos, downloads, and other resources.

By using Bible Mapped, you agree to use the site and its content in a way that honors God, respects the work, and helps others study Scripture with humility and care.

1. Our Purpose

Bible Mapped exists to help people see the Bible in context.

We focus on the where, the who, the what, and the why of Scripture. We believe the Bible was written through real people, in real places, across real history, all inspired by a real God.

Our goal is not to replace Scripture. Our goal is to help people slow down, study carefully, and see how the Bible connects across place, time, culture, meaning, and Jesus Christ.

You may quote small portions of our content, share links to our pages, and use our resources to help explain Scripture, as long as you credit Bible Mapped and do not present the work as your own.

When sharing, please include credit like:

Website: biblemapped.com

We encourage every reader to compare what they read with Scripture, pray for wisdom, and continue studying.

Bible Mapped is a study resource. It is not the final authority. Scripture is.
