What is the Bible?

The Bible is the inspired Word of God, written through real people, in real places, across real history.

It is not a random collection of religious writings. It is one unfolding story of creation, covenant, promise, redemption, and the kingdom of God, all pointing to Jesus Christ.

What is a map?

A map helps you see where something happened and how it connects to everything around it.

At Bible Mapped, a map is more than a picture of land. It is a way of seeing Scripture in context. We map the where, the who, the what, and the why so the Bible becomes clearer, deeper, and more connected.
