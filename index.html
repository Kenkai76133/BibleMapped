Mission

The Bible does not hang above history.

It unfolds within history — through real places, real people, real timelines, and real meaning. Bible Mapped was created to help people see the world of Scripture and how the whole story points to Jesus Christ.

From Eden to Abraham, from Egypt to Sinai, from Jerusalem to the cross, and from the empty tomb to the hope of all things — the Bible tells a story grounded in place, context, and meaning.

Follow the trail of Scripture.

Bible Mapped helps Christians and seekers ask better questions: Where did it happen? Who was involved? When did it happen? And why does it still matter?
