Interactive structure

A mind map built out of books.

Click the Bible to open the Old Testament and New Testament. Click the Old Testament to open the book groups. Click a group to explore books. Click a book to see its meaning, theme, major characters, and how it points to Jesus.

What is the Bible?

The Bible is a unified collection of writings that reveal God’s relationship with humanity through creation, covenant, redemption, and fulfillment.
