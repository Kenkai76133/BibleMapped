Why This Matters

The Bible wasn’t written in abstraction. It unfolded across real terrain, real cities, and real empires. When geography is restored, meaning sharpens.

Real Places

Scripture happened in specific locations — deserts, rivers, trade routes, and borders.

Real Movement

Journeys, exile, conquest, and return all follow geographic logic.

Real Meaning

Understanding where events happened changes how we understand why they happened.
