The Bible Mapped Framework

Understand Scripture through place, people, time, meaning, and Jesus.

Every passage happened somewhere, involved someone, took place in a real moment, carried meaning, and ultimately fits into the larger story of Christ.

Places

Where did it happen? Geography helps us see the Bible inside real history.

People

Who was involved? Scripture is full of real lives, choices, families, kings, prophets, and witnesses.

Time

When did it happen? Context helps us avoid reading the Bible like it happened in a vacuum.

Meaning

Why does it matter? The Bible gives us truth, wisdom, warning, hope, and instruction.

Jesus

How does it point to Christ? Scripture’s story finds its center in Him.
