Canon / Books

Open the Bible by section.

The Bible is easier to study when the books are grouped by function. Choose Old Testament, New Testament, or the books received differently across Christian traditions.

Old Testament

The covenant Scriptures before the coming of Christ. These books give the foundation: creation, promise, law, wisdom, prophecy, exile, and hope.

New Testament

The writings centered on Jesus Christ, His life, death, resurrection, the apostles, the church, and the hope of new creation.

Non-canonical / Deuterocanonical

Books received differently across Christian traditions. Bible Mapped labels them clearly so readers know how Protestant, Catholic, and Orthodox canons differ.
